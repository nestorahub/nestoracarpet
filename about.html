Our Story

Since 1990, Nestorahub has redefined carpet excellence in the UAE, establishing itself as the nation’s premier supplier of premium carpets. From our headquarters in Dubai’s Business Bay, we’ve transformed countless homes, offices, and mosques with our unparalleled range of wall-to-wall, carpet tiles, mosque, shaggy, and more. Our commitment to superior quality, innovative designs, and eco-friendly materials sets us apart as the industry leader.

At Nestorahub, we don’t just supply carpets—we create lasting impressions. With free delivery and installation across the UAE, our passion for perfection ensures every project reflects our unmatched expertise.
